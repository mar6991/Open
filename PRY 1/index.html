De donde venimos

Our posturing, our imagined self-importance,
the desilusion that we have some privileged
position in the Universe, are challenged by this
point of pale light.
Our planet is lonely speck in the great
enveloping cosmic dark. In our obscurity, in all
this vastness, there is not hint that help will
come from elsewhere to save us from ourselves.
